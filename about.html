Who I am

I'm a nurse. I work 12-hour shifts. I spend those hours keeping people alive — which is meaningful, demanding, and not something that earns money when I'm not there.

A few years ago I started thinking about what happens when I get sick, burned out, or just want to take three weeks off without financial panic. The answer was: income that doesn't require my presence.

What this site is

I'm building 10 income streams in public. Every week I publish what's actually earning, what's not, and what I'm doing differently. Real numbers, not projections.

The goal: $100 per night from passive income — $36,500 per year — without quitting my job or working a second one.

The streams I'm building: DeFi yield, Etsy digital downloads, Amazon KDP, print-on-demand, affiliate SEO, a paid newsletter, YouTube, eBay arbitrage, options wheel strategy, and lead generation.

Why "Money Psychology"?

Because most of what stops people from building multiple income streams isn't knowledge — it's psychology. The fear of starting. The belief that income streams are for "other people." The habit of thinking in terms of hourly pay instead of assets.

I was one of those people. This site is where I document changing that.

What you'll find here

Honest guides on the income streams I'm building — real steps, real numbers, honest risks. If something doesn't work, I'll tell you that too. The point isn't to impress you. It's to show you a real process.
