Look up the GSA (General Services Administration) per diem rate for any U.S. city. Travel nurses use this to verify their housing stipend qualifies as tax-free.

Why this matters: Your housing stipend must be at or below the GSA rate to qualify as tax-free income. If your agency pays above GSA, the excess is taxable. If they pay below, you're leaving money on the table.
