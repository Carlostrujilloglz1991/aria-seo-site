Nursing School Supplies List 2026: Everything You Actually Need (Budget & Premium Picks)

Real talk: You don't need everything on every "nursing school supplies" list you'll find online. This guide tells you what to buy, what to skip, and gives you both a budget pick and a premium pick for each category.

Starting nursing school is expensive enough before you start panic-buying gear. We surveyed working nurses and nursing students to separate the essentials from the overpriced fluff. Every item on this list earns its place — and we'll tell you exactly where to save money and where spending more actually matters.

7. Nursing Diagnosis Handbook ESSENTIAL

You'll write dozens of care plans with this

Nursing diagnosis handbooks are required for nearly every care plan you write in nursing school. They list NANDA diagnoses, related factors, defining characteristics, suggested outcomes (NOC), and nursing interventions (NIC). One reference covers your whole program.

Budget Pick

Nursing Diagnosis Handbook: An Evidence-Based Guide (Ackley & Ladwig) — older edition
Difference between 11th and 12th edition is minimal for care plans. Used copies of the 11th Ed. are $10–$20.

$10–$20 (used)
