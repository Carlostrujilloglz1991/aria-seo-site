The Nurse Side Hustle & Passive Income Hub — Organized by What Each One Costs You

Every nurse income idea sits somewhere on one axis: how much of your remaining time and energy it consumes. Picking up shifts pays immediately and costs the most; a digital product costs a lot up front and then almost nothing. Neither end is better — they solve different problems — but mixing them up is how nurses end up burned out twice. This hub organizes every income guide on the site along that axis, from trading hours at a premium to building assets that pay while you sleep.

How to use this page: Be honest about which resource you have spare — time or patience. If you need money this month, start in the trading-hours section. If you can wait six months for a payoff, start in the digital-assets section. Whatever you pick, read the tax section before the first dollar arrives; 1099 income surprises more nurses than night shift ever did.
