What to Look for in Any Nursing Log Book

Lay-flat binding: You're writing on the go, often standing. A book that stays open without you holding it down is worth the extra $2.

Pre-formatted columns: Generic lined journals don't work for clinical data. You want columns matched to your specialty's actual workflow.

6×9 size: Fits in a white coat pocket or scrub pocket. Anything bigger stays in the locker and doesn't get used.

Enough pages: A 50-page book lasts 3 months. A 120-page book lasts a year. Buy once.
