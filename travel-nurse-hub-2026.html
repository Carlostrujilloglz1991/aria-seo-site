The Travel Nursing Hub — From First Contract to Tax Season, Mapped

Travel nursing is really three skill sets stacked on top of your clinical one: picking agencies and contracts like a negotiator, running stipends and housing like a small business, and staying clean with the IRS. This site has built guides for each layer, one contract question at a time, and this hub organizes all of them in the order a traveler actually meets the problems — from "am I ready?" to "where did my stipend go?" to the audit letter you hope never comes.

How to use this page: If you are pre-first-contract, read the getting-started section top to bottom — it is a self-directed orientation. If you are already on the road, jump to the section holding this month's problem. Taxes have their own dedicated hub linked below, including the state-by-state library. New travel guides get added here as they publish.
