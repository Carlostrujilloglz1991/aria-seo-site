ICU nurses earn more than general floor nurses in virtually every hospital in the US. The salary premium for critical care skills is real, measurable, and — if you play the travel nurse market correctly — can push a single RN past $150,000 per year with overtime. Here's the full breakdown for 2026.

The real number most ICU nurses hit: Staff nurses working a mix of regular shifts + voluntary overtime at a well-paying hospital land around $95,000–$110,000 in total compensation including shift differentials. Travel nurses in the same specialty earn $130,000–$155,000 doing identical clinical work.
