Nurse CEU Requirements by State 2026

CEU requirements vary widely — from 0 hours (some states) to 30+ hours per renewal cycle. Check your specific state board before your renewal deadline.

Mandatory Topic Requirements

Many states require specific content regardless of total hours. Common mandates: pain management and end-of-life care (CA, PA, many others), opioid awareness training (PA, OH, NJ), HIV/AIDS education (FL), child abuse recognition and reporting (multiple states), human trafficking awareness (multiple states, increasingly common).

Free CEU Sources for Nurses

  • Nurse.com — offers free and paid CEUs, large library
  • Medscape Nursing — free CE with account, many clinical topics
  • ANA (American Nurses Association) — member access to CE library
  • AACN — critical care CNEs, many free with member discount
  • CDC TRAIN — free public health CEUs (infection control, immunizations)
  • Your employer — most hospitals offer free CEUs via their education department or online learning management system
Travel nurse tip: Compact license states (NLC) still require you to maintain CEU compliance with your HOME state requirements — not the state where you're working. Keep a log of all CEUs completed with dates and provider names.
