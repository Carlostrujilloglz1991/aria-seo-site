UWorld: The Gold Standard

UWorld remains the most recommended NCLEX prep tool by nursing educators and repeat test-takers. The questions are harder than the actual exam — which means if you're scoring 55–60% on UWorld in test mode, you are likely ready to pass. Focus on reading every single rationale, not just your wrong answers.

Archer Review: Best Budget Option

Archer has published strong internal pass rates and is particularly well-aligned with the NGN format. At $99–$199, it's the best value if you can't justify UWorld pricing. Many nurses combine Archer + free Mark Klimek lectures for a complete low-cost prep stack.

Strategy: Do NOT buy multiple full courses. Pick one question bank (UWorld or Archer), do 75–100 questions per day, and read every rationale. Volume beats variety.

What Not to Buy

Avoid expensive "live bootcamp" programs unless your school already covers them. Most charge $500–$1,000 for content you can get from UWorld + free YouTube. Also avoid any program making "guaranteed pass" claims — these are marketing, not guarantees.

  • Nurse Sarah YouTube channel (NCLEX pharmacology mnemonics)
  • Picmonic — visual mnemonics (good supplement, not standalone)
